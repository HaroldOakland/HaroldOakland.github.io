Here's a bit about myself


Pluto is a cold, distant world...well, mini world...but I wouldn't be who I am if I weren't nurtured in the frigid bussom of the methane ice caves our people eventually settled. Once I moved to Earth, I discovered sweating and let me tell you, I hate it. However, there aren't any tamales at the poles either, so I live in Denver. There aren't many tamales here, nor any methane caves, but I like it here.


I grok with a woman who is my wife and we enjoy multiple activities and of course, good alcoholic beverages. Now I find myself pecking away at a keyboard attempting to learn how to make graphical interfaces for users to interact with so I can make some money.


I know I'll never return to the desolate methane tundra of Pluto, and I know that I do not want to. However, I do still miss carving out reliefs on the cave walls using only my warm breath. Feel free to head over to the contact form and leave a message!
